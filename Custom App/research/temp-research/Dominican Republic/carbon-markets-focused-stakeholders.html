Carbon Markets Focus Summary

Research Scope: This analysis focuses exclusively on stakeholders directly involved in Voluntary Carbon Markets (VCM), Carbon Tax implementation, Emissions Trading Systems (ETS), and Paris Agreement Article 6.2 (ITMOs) and 6.4 (PACM) mechanisms in the Dominican Republic.

Verified Carbon Market Mechanisms Active in DR:

  • Article 6.2 ITMOs: Bilateral cooperation agreement with Singapore for carbon credit trading
  • Article 6.4 PACM: 60+ projects in national CDM office, transitioning to new centralized mechanism
  • VCM Projects: Framework established but no confirmed operational credit generators verified
  • Carbon Tax: Implemented CO2 emission tax on motor vehicles via DGII
  • REDD+ ERPA: $25M results-based payment agreement with World Bank for forest carbon
